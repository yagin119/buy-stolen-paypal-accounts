Prevention is the best defense. Here's a checklist to safeguard your PayPal and other accounts:

- **Enable MFA**: Use app-based authenticators instead of SMS for added security.

- **Avoid Phishing**: Verify emails and links before clicking; official PayPal communications never ask for login details.
